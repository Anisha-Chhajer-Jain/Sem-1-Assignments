DELHI

Delhi is the capital city of India. It is a vibrant blend of history and modernity. Known for its rich cultural heritage, it houses iconic landmarks like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, India Gate, and Humayun’s Tomb. The city is also a political hub, home to the Indian Parliament and Rashtrapati Bhavan. Bustling markets, diverse cuisines, and rapid urban growth make Delhi a lively and dynamic metropolis that reflects India’s past and present together. networks, shopping malls, and busy roads. Being the political center of the country, Delhi is home to important buildings like the Parliament House, Rashtrapati Bhavan, and the Supreme Court. People from different parts of India live here, which makes Delhi a city of diverse cultures, languages, and traditions. The vibrant markets of Chandni Chowk, Connaught Place, and Sarojini Nagar attract thousands of visitors every day, while the city’s street food like chaat, parathas, and golgappas are loved by all. Delhi also plays a major role in education and employment. It has some of the best universities and institutions, such as Delhi University, JNU, and AIIMS. Many people come here for better opportunities and to fulfill their dreams.
