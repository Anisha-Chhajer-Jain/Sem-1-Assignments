PIZZA

Pizza is a famous Italian dish that is enjoyed all over the world. It is made with a soft, round base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and different toppings like vegetables or meat. The pizza is baked in an oven until the crust is golden and the cheese melts. With its crispy base and cheesy flavor, pizza is a favorite food for parties, celebrations, and family meals.


Ingredients:

-2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
-1 teaspoon yeast
-1 teaspoon sugar
-½ teaspoon salt
-2 tablespoons oil
-½ cup warm water (to knead dough)
-½ cup pizza sauce (or tomato ketchup)
-1 cup grated cheese (mozzarella or processed)
-Vegetables like capsicum, onion, tomato, corn, or olives

Method:

Mix yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let it rest for 5–10 minutes until frothy.
Add flour, salt, and oil. Knead into a soft dough and let it rise for 1–2 hours.
Roll the dough into a round base and place it on a greased baking tray.
Spread pizza sauce over the base, add vegetables, and sprinkle cheese on top.
Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 15–20 minutes, until the cheese melts and the crust turns golden.
Slice and serve hot.

Cooking Terms:


Dough – A soft mixture of flour, water, and yeast used as the pizza base.

Knead – To press and fold dough until it becomes smooth.

Roll – To flatten dough into a round shape using a rolling pin.

Bake – To cook pizza in an oven.

Slice – To cut pizza into pieces for serving.
