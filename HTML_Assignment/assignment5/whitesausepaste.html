WHITE SAUSE PASTA

White sauce pasta is a creamy and delicious dish made with cooked pasta mixed in a smooth white sauce. The sauce is prepared using butter, flour, and milk, and is flavored with cheese, garlic, and herbs. Vegetables like capsicum, corn, or broccoli can be added to make it healthier and more colorful. Loved by children and adults alike, white sauce pasta is a quick meal that can be enjoyed for lunch, dinner, or even as a snack. Its rich and cheesy taste makes it one of the most popular pasta dishes.


Ingredients:

-2 cups pasta (any type)
-2 cups water (to boil pasta)
-2 tablespoons butter
-2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (maida)
-2 cups milk
-½ cup grated cheese
-½ teaspoon black pepper
-Salt to taste
-Vegetables (capsicum, corn, broccoli, carrot – optional)

Method:

Boil pasta in water with a little salt until soft. Drain and keep aside.
In a pan, melt butter and add flour. Stir for 1 minute to remove raw taste.
Slowly add milk while stirring to make a smooth, thick sauce.
Add cheese, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
Add boiled pasta (and vegetables if using) into the sauce. Stir until coated.
Serve hot and creamy.

Cooking Terms:

Boil – To cook pasta in hot water until soft.

Drain – To remove water from boiled pasta.

Melt – To heat butter until it becomes liquid.

Stir – To mix ingredients in a circular motion.

Simmer – To cook gently on low heat.

Grate – To shred cheese into small pieces.

✨ Now you have delicious White Sause Pasta ready!
