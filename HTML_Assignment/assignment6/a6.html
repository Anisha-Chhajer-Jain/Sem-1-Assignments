Class 10 - Report Card

A report card is an official record of a student’s performance in school.
It shows the marks or grades obtained in different subjects, along with remarks from teachers about progress and behavior.
Report cards help students understand their strengths and areas of improvement. They are also a way for parents to know about their child’s learning and overall development.
Receiving a good report card encourages students to work harder and do even better in their studies.
